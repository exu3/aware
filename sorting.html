Sorting

Instructions: Welcome to sorting! Once you click start, there will be a task where you will drag words from the word bank to each respective category. Then, once you finish the first part, you will move on to the second part where you’ll be given slightly different categories. You will sort these once again. When sorting, you must start from the first card in the top left and continue to the right. After you are finished sorting, there will be a page that allows you to reflect on the activity and your biases.
